Founders' Story

We are Ari, Nancy, and Ben â€” a brother and sister-in-law caregiving team. Ben lives with an ultra-rare condition called TUBB4A-related leukodystrophy. As his condition progressed, he became nonspeaking and quadriplegic, leaving him with very limited options for interaction.

When we searched for tools to help Ben, most focused only on basic communication. Ben wanted more â€” to play games, browse the internet, watch his favorite shows, and truly connect with the world. So, we built our own custom two-button system that Ben controls with his head.

Our focus is now on sharing these games and tools, optimized specifically for one and two button users, so others can find the same joy and connection.

Support the Mission

Your support helps make access possible for people who are often left out of technology, play, and expression. We focus on building and supporting simple, usable tools that work with limited input and real world constraints.

Your support helps us:

  • Develop accessible games and tools where options are limited
  • Maintain and grow shared libraries of accessible tools and games
  • Develop, test, and support simple, low cost and DIY hardware solutions
  • Support open, low cost accessibility projects and collaborators
  • Cover essential infrastructure such as hosting and software
  • Sustain this work over time

The NARBE Foundation is a registered nonprofit. Donations are used to directly support our programs and the people doing the work behind them.
