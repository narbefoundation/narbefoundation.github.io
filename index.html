Founders' Story

We are Ari, Nancy, and Ben — a brother and sister-in-law caregiving team.

Ben lives with an ultra-rare condition called TUBB4A-related leukodystrophy. As his condition progressed, he became nonverbal and quadriplegic, leaving him with very limited options for interaction. His mind, however, remains sharp and full of life.

When we searched for tools to help Ben, most focused only on basic communication. They were slow to get, costly, and limited in what they offered. Ben wanted more — to play games, browse the internet, watch his favorite shows, and truly connect with the world.

So, we built our own. Using creativity and AI, we designed a custom two-button system that Ben controls with his head. Now he can type, communicate, watch shows and movies on any streaming platform, search YouTube and Google Images, and play custom-built games — all through one simple, accessible hub.

Our journey showed us what's possible when families and technology come together to create tools built not just for basic needs, but for connection and joy.

You are not alone.

We understand the feeling of being stuck, wondering if anyone will help, and searching for hope.

We've been exactly where you are. The countless hours researching solutions that don't quite fit. The frustration of expensive tools that promise everything but deliver limitations. The feeling that your loved one might never have the access they deserve.

But here's what we've learned: with today's rapidly evolving technology — especially AI — families no longer have to wait for someone else to build the perfect solution. We can take the power into our own hands and create exactly what our loved ones need.

What seemed impossible just a few years ago is now within reach for any determined family. You don't need to be a programmer or engineer. You just need the willingness to learn, experiment, and build something meaningful together.

Our story with Ben is proof that extraordinary things happen when love meets innovation. We're here to show you what's possible and help you discover the tools and confidence to create your own breakthrough moments.

Mission in Action

We had no background in software development — only a need to give Ben a way to connect with the world. With the help of AI, we learned step by step how to build completely custom tools for him.

Now, with just two buttons, Ben can:

• Watch his favorite shows and movies on any streaming platform

• Search YouTube and Google Images

• Play custom-built games made just for him

• Type and communicate with a predictive keyboard and phrase boards

• Control computer settings

• Ask questions, play music, and get answers through a voice assistant

What started as a solution for Ben has grown into a mission to help others. Families and professionals shouldn't be held back by high costs, long wait times, or one-size-fits-all tools.

We share our tools, guides, and games freely so others can create solutions tailored to their own needs. AI can speed up the process, but it's never required — the focus is on giving people access to powerful, affordable tools that make life easier.

Start a campaign. Our operational costs are low, but with additional funding, we can do extraordinary things:

  • Provide tablets and hardware to families who need access but can't afford the tools
