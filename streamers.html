Gaming for Everyone

Play is a universal language. We help make it accessible to all.

Our Philosophy

Gaming isn't just about entertainment; it's about connection, challenge, and joy. For many people with disabilities, traditional controllers are a barrier to entry.

One and Two Button Gaming

We focus on "one and two button" games. By simplifying the controls, we open up a world of play to those who might only have reliable control over a single switch. This approach allows for immediate fun without the steep learning curve of complex adaptive setups.

Our flagship project, Benny's Hub, is a collection of web-based games designed specifically for this purpose. It's free, open, and ready to play in your browser.

Play Benny's Hub →

Complex Setups & Adaptive Controllers

We specialize in the simple, immediate access side of things. However, many gamers need more complex setups involving eye-tracking, voice control, or custom controller mapping.

For these needs, we highly recommend the following organizations who are experts in the field:

SpecialEffect

A UK-based charity that puts fun and inclusion back into the lives of people with physical disabilities by helping them to play video games.

Visit SpecialEffect →

AbleGamers

Creating opportunities that enable play in order to combat social isolation, foster inclusive communities, and improve the quality of life for people with disabilities.

Visit AbleGamers →

OneSwitch

A resource for switch accessible games and information.

Visit OneSwitch →
